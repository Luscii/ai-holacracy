# Roadmap

This roadmap sequences the problems in `ISSUE-TREE.md` toward the vision's three-agent plugin — Coach, Editor, Judge — under the boundary *advise · author · adjudicate, never execute*. The ordering starts from the substrate all three agents stand on: a grounded, resolvable Constitution. VISION success criterion 3 ("every ruling grounded in the Constitution") and the machine-checkable-text constraint make that foundation the prerequisite for any non-fabricated output, which is why it leads.

From there the sequence leans into the Editor, where the most work already exists (two quality skills) and the origin tension lives — weak governance artifacts — before opening the Judge (which verifies the Editor's output) and the Coach (which gets newcomers effective faster). Relationships in the issue tree are treated as soft context here, not build-order; cross-problem build-order surfaces later in `score:prioritize` once a problem is committed in `score:model`.

## Now

1. **Ungrounded or Unresolvable Constitution** — Ungrounded or Unresolvable Constitution — The foundation every agent grounds in; without a single resolvable Constitution, citations can't be checked and verdicts risk resting on invented text (VISION success 3; No Fabricated Citations).
2. **Change-set Coherence** — Low-Quality Governance Artifacts — The fresh Editor gap that builds on the existing purpose/accountability skills and reaches a valid change-set without a human coach (VISION success 2); also what the Judge's Validity check verifies.
